The Owner

Hi, I'm Noelle! I took my first ceramics class in high school and really struggled. I was determined to succeed, so I came into the school's studio before school, after school, and at lunch until I started figuring it out. I owe much of where I am to a very patient teacher who recognized my desire to learn and helped me get there. By the end of that semester, I actually enjoyed woking with the clay! I decided to take the more advanced class the following year, and was really able to cultivate my talent.

My freshman year of college I took yet another ceramics class. This one pushed me to do more than I believed I was capable of. I've always loved the process that takes a ball of clay and transforms it into something beautiful and unique. It was in this class I knew I wanted a studio of my own. I spent as much time as possible throwing on the wheel, building by hand, experimenting with glazes, and pushing the limits of what I was capable of. Now, I want to help others recognize their abilities and create their own pieces.

The Dream

  1. A studio of my own

  2. A place to share what I love
